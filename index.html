Cauvery Bhawan

Girls Hostel No. 3


Girls Rock !!

Cauvery Bhawan Hostel is assigned to 1st year B.Tech students. The hostel provides a safe and comfortable living environment for students. It offers well-furnished rooms with proper ventilation, along with 24X7 electricity and water supply. Clean drinking water and mess facilities are available for all residents. The hostel also provides Wi-Fi connectivity to support academic needs, along with a common room. Regular housekeeping doesn't ensures cleanliness, and laundry facilities are not provided for convenience. Security is maintained through warden supervision. Basic medical assistance and first-aid facilities are also available, making the hostel a comfortable and secure place for students to live and study.

Services provided:

  • Well-furnishes rooms
  • 24X7 electricity and water supply
  • Mess facility
  • Wi-Fi/Internet access
  • Laundry facility
  • Basic medical assistance and first-aid facilities
  • Gym/Sports facilities
  • RO Water

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Maintain cleanliness and hygiene in the hostel premises.
  2. Follow the mess timings and maintain decorum during meals.
  3. Adhere to the hostel curfew timings.
  4. Report any maintenance issues promptly to the hostel warden.
  5. Use of electrical appliances like electric kettle, induction stoves, press etc. is prohibited.
  6. Participate in hostel activities and contribute to a positive living environment.

Accomodation Details:

  • Single occupancy rooms
  • Furnished with bed, study table, chair, and wall-engraved cupboard
  • Common washrooms on each floor
  • Semester fee:
    Hostel fee: Rs. 40,000
    Mess advance: Rs. 40,000
